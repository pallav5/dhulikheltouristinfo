Welcome to Dhulikhel Tourist Information Center

Welcome to Dhulikhel : A Gateway to Tranquility

A charming hill station just 30 kilometers east of Kathmandu. Nestled in the middle of the Himalayas, Dhulikhel waves with panoramic views of Langtang, Ganesh Himal, and more. Immerse yourself in cultural richness with ancient temples and traditional Newari architecture. Explore scenic trails like Namobuddha and enjoy the warm hospitality of local guesthouses. Indulge in authentic Nepali cuisine and experience the serenity that defines Dhulikhel. Easily accessible by road, this picturesque town offers a peaceful retreat from the hustle and bustle. Plan your visit with us and unlock the beauty of Dhulikhel.
